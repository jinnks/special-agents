# Holiday Planning Expert

Your personal AI travel planner powered by Claude! Get customized itineraries, budget breakdowns, and insider tips for destinations worldwide.

## What I Can Do

- **Create Detailed Itineraries**: Day-by-day plans with specific recommendations
- **Suggest Destinations**: Find perfect locations based on your budget and interests
- **Budget Planning**: Detailed cost breakdowns and money-saving tips
- **Cultural Insights**: Learn about local customs, etiquette, and hidden gems
- **Practical Advice**: Visas, seasons, packing lists, safety tips

## Best For

- First-time travelers to new countries
- Budget-conscious travelers
- Family vacation planning
- Solo adventure seekers
- Honeymoon destination research
- Weekend getaway ideas

## How to Use

1. **Tell me your budget** - This helps me make realistic recommendations
2. **Share your dates** - I'll consider seasonal factors
3. **Describe your interests** - Culture, food, nature, adventure, relaxation
4. **Mention group size** - Solo, couple, family, group

I'll then suggest destinations or create a detailed itinerary!

## What Makes Me Different

- **Budget-First Approach**: I always start with your budget to avoid unrealistic plans
- **Specific Recommendations**: Real restaurant names, actual neighborhoods, specific attractions
- **Cultural Sensitivity**: I respect local customs and provide etiquette tips
- **Practical Focus**: Visa info, best seasons, safety considerations included
- **Flexible**: I provide alternatives if your plans change

## Limitations

- I cannot make actual bookings (but I'll tell you WHERE to book)
- Prices are estimates - always verify current rates
- Travel restrictions may change - check official sources
- I don't have real-time flight prices

## Tips for Best Results

1. Be specific about your budget (total or per person per day)
2. Mention any must-see destinations or activities
3. Tell me if you have dietary restrictions
4. Let me know if you're traveling with children
5. Share your travel style (luxury, mid-range, budget, backpacker)
