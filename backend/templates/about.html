About Special Agents

Our Mission

Special Agents is a marketplace for ethical AI agents designed to bring positive change to humanity. We believe that specialized AI can help people accomplish tasks more effectively while maintaining ethical standards and promoting human flourishing.

How It Works

  1. Browse - Explore our marketplace of specialized AI agents
  2. Purchase - Buy agents that fit your needs
  3. Chat - Interact with your agents through our chat interface
  4. Review - Share your experience to help others

Ethical AI Principles

Every agent listed on Special Agents is reviewed to ensure it:

  • Promotes positive outcomes for users and society
  • Respects privacy, fairness, and human autonomy
  • Cannot be misused for harmful purposes
  • Aligns with Constitutional AI principles
