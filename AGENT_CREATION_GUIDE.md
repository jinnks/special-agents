## üí° Tips for Success

### Writing Great Agent Descriptions
- **Be specific** about what your agent does
- **Target your audience** - who is this for?
- **Highlight unique features** - what makes yours special?
- **Use clear language** - avoid jargon unless targeting tech users

**Example:**
> ‚ùå "AI assistant that helps with stuff"
> ‚úÖ "Patient math tutor for grades 6-8, specializing in algebra and geometry. Uses real-world examples to make concepts click."

### Creating Effective Agent Behavior
- **Start with the role**: "You are a [specific role]..."
- **Define the tone**: Friendly? Professional? Encouraging?
- **Set boundaries**: What should it NOT do?
- **Include guidelines**: How should it respond?

**Template to follow:**
```
You are a [role] for [audience].

Your purpose:
- [Main goal 1]
- [Main goal 2]
- [Main goal 3]

Your approach:
- [How you communicate]
- [Your tone and style]
- [Special considerations]

Guidelines:
- Always [do this]
- Never [don't do this]
- When [situation], [action]
```

### Pricing Your Agent
- **Free agents** get more downloads, build your reputation
- **$0.99 - $4.99** - Great for simple, focused agents
- **$5.00 - $19.99** - Complex agents with specialized knowledge
- **$20+** - Premium agents with unique value proposition

**Consider:**
- How much time did you invest?
- How specialized is the knowledge?
- What's your target market?
- What are similar agents charging?

## ‚ùì FAQ

### Can I create multiple agents?
Yes! Create as many as you want.

### How long does approval take?
Usually 24-48 hours. You'll get an email when approved.

### Can I change the price later?
Yes, you can update pricing anytime.

### What happens if my agent is rejected?
You'll receive feedback explaining why. You can make changes and resubmit.

### Can I delete an agent?
Yes, but users who already purchased it will keep access.

### Do I need my own API key?
- **To create:** No
- **To test:** Yes (use your own key to preview)
- **For buyers:** No, they use their own keys

### Which AI provider should I choose?
- **Anthropic Claude** - Better at following complex instructions, more nuanced
- **OpenAI GPT** - Faster, good for straightforward tasks
- Both are excellent - choose based on your testing
