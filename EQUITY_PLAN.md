## üìã Vesting Schedule

### Standard Terms

**All equity vests over 4 years:**

**Year 1:**
- **Cliff:** No equity vests for first 12 months
- **After 12 months:** 25% vests immediately
- **Purpose:** Ensures commitment

**Years 2-4:**
- Quarterly vesting (6.25% every 3 months)
- 36 months of quarterly vests
- Total: 75% vests over 36 months

**Example (2% grant):**
```
Month 0-11:   0%     (cliff period)
Month 12:     0.5%   (25% cliff vest)
Month 15:     0.125% (quarterly)
Month 18:     0.125% (quarterly)
...
Month 48:     2%     (fully vested)
```

### Accelerated Vesting

**Triggers:**
- Company acquisition: 50% immediate vest
- IPO: 25% immediate vest
- Termination without cause: 6 months accelerated

## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: Is this legally binding?**
A: Equity grants require signed agreements. Verbal/GitHub commitments are not binding until paperwork is signed.

**Q: What happens if I leave before 4 years?**
A: You keep vested equity. Unvested equity is forfeited (unless special circumstances).

**Q: Can I sell my equity?**
A: Not until exit event (acquisition/IPO). Right of first refusal applies.

**Q: What's the company structure?**
A: LLC initially, converting to C-Corp when needed for funding.

**Q: Do I pay anything for equity?**
A: No. Your contribution is your "payment" for equity.

**Q: What if company fails?**
A: Equity becomes worthless. This is startup risk.

**Q: What about taxes?**
A: Consult tax advisor. In US, equity compensation may be taxable. 83(b) election may apply.
